phpMailMerge is a PHP script to send a serial letter in MIME format. Recipients are taken from tagged entries of a CSV database. Depending on the language, a German and English version will be generated. In addition to the HTML message, the email also contains an alternative plain text message. If attachments are defined, they will be added.
